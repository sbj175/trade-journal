# OPT-180: Feature backlog: data quality, analysis, workflow, and polish improvements

Collected feature ideas and gaps identified during development. Break into sub-issues as priorities emerge.

## Data Quality

* **Custom strategy labeling** — "Custom (2-leg)", "Custom (3-leg)" groups mean the recognition engine didn't match. Add a workflow to manually label these or teach the engine new patterns.
* **Shares / wheel integration** — Shares groups have no direction/type metadata. Link share assignments back to the put that was assigned to support wheel strategy tracking.

## Analysis

* **Trade journal notes per group** — Persistent notes on Ledger groups explaining *why* you entered a trade. The `OrderComment` model exists but is underused. This is the core value prop of a trade journal.
* **Cumulative P&L / equity curve** — Reports has monthly bars but no cumulative equity curve showing drawdowns and growth trajectory over time.
* **Expected value vs actual** — Compare max risk/reward (already calculated) against actual P&L to highlight early exits and management decisions.

## Workflow

* **Strategy targets UI** — `StrategyTarget` model exists in the schema but isn't surfaced in the UI. Set profit/loss targets per group and track against them.
* **Expiration calendar** — Calendar view showing upcoming expirations across all open positions. Positions page has DTE but no visual timeline.
* **Roll tracking visibility** — Rolls are detected in the pipeline but the Ledger doesn't clearly show "this group was rolled from X to Y" as a visual progression.

## Polish

* **Mobile / responsive layout** — Ledger columns assume a wide viewport; needs responsive breakpoints.
* **CSV/PDF export** — No export for tax reporting or external analysis.
* **Dashboard landing page** — Currently `/` redirects to Positions. A dashboard combining key metrics (open P&L, expirations this week, win rate trend) would be a better home page.
