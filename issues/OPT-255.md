# OPT-255: Landing: adopt tastytrade lowercase brand convention (hybrid)

After researching tastytrade's own brand style (their website, press releases, and support articles all use lowercase "tastytrade"), switch from the current "always-capitalize" convention to a hybrid approach: lowercase in body copy, capital "T" only at sentence starts and headings where the word begins the heading.

Supersedes [OPT-254](https://linear.app/optionedge/issue/OPT-254/landing-normalize-tastytrade-capitalization) (which normalized everything to capital T).

## Plan

* Replace "Tastytrade" → "tastytrade" across `landing/index.html`
* Manually restore capital at sentence starts (one known case: "Tastytrade OAuth with no..." in the Security section)
* Update feedback memory to reflect the new convention so future copy work follows it

## Why

Matches the brand owner's own styling, signals brand literacy, and is what Apple-style brand manuals (iPhone, adidas-adjacent) would recommend.
