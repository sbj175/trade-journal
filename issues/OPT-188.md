# OPT-188: Bear ZEBRA being recognized as a Bull ZEBRA

QQQ short 1 467 put, long 2 483 puts

That position is a **bearish ZEBRA**.

Let’s break it down.

### Your Position

* **Short 1 × 467 put**
* **Long 2 × 483 puts**

All else equal, the **483 puts are higher-strike** and therefore **deeper in-the-money** than the 467 put.

### ZEBRA Concept

A **ZEBRA (Zero Extrinsic Back Ratio)** is designed so that:

* The **extrinsic value of the 2 long options ≈ extrinsic of the 1 short option**
* Result: **near-zero time decay**
* Net position behaves like **\~100 shares of stock (delta ≈ ±1)**

### Direction Determination

For **puts**:

* **Long puts → bearish**
* **Short puts → bullish**

But you have **2 long puts vs 1 short put**, and the **longs are higher strike**, so the net exposure is **strongly bearish**.

### Payoff Intuition

If the underlying **falls**:

* The **483 puts gain value faster**
* The **467 short put loses value (good for you)** until price drops below 467

So the trade profits most if the **stock moves downward**.

### Rule of Thumb

* **ZEBRA using calls → bullish**
* **ZEBRA using puts → bearish**

---

✅ **Answer: Bearish ZEBRA**
