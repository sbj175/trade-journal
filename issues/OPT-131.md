# OPT-131: Fix strategy label for rolled spreads in closed groups

**Bug:** When a closed group contains a rolled spread (e.g., a bull call spread at 177.5/190 rolled up to 192.5/205), the strategy label shows "Custom (4-leg)" instead of "Bull Call Spread".\\n\\n**Root cause:** `_label_from_all_lots()` in `group_manager.py` builds synthetic legs from ALL lots in the group — including the closed-out legs from before the roll. The strategy engine sees 4 distinct legs at 4 different strikes and doesn't recognize the pattern.\\n\\n**Fix:** When labeling a closed group, use only the lots from the most recent opening order (the final position shape) rather than all historical lots.\\n\\n**Example:** ALB in account 5WZ26959 — bull call spread rolled from 177.5/190 to 192.5/205, same expiration. Was labeled "Custom (4-leg)", now correctly labeled "Bull Call Spread".</n\\n**Reported by user on account 5WZ26959, symbol ALB.**"
