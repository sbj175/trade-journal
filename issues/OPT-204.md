# OPT-204: Ledger: Consolidate lots by strike

Within a Ledger group, multiple lots exist at the same strike due to split fills. This creates visual clutter. Consolidate into one row per (expiration + strike + option_type) combination with aggregated quantity and weighted-average entry price. Also remove move-lots functionality (no longer needed).
