Great question — this is one of those “simple once you know the pieces” setups. You basically need email hosting + DNS configuration for your domain (**optionledger.app**). Here’s the clean way to do it.

### 🧩 Step 1: Choose an Email Hosting Provider

Your domain host (where you bought optionledger.app) does NOT automatically give you email — you need a service.

* **Google Workspace:** \~$6/month per user (Gmail interface, most common).
* **Microsoft 365:** Outlook-based.
* **Zoho Mail:** Has a free tier (good for small setups).
* **Proton Mail:** Privacy-focused.

  **Recommendation:** Use **Google Workspace** for ease/reliability or **Zoho** if you want to stay free/cheap.

### ⚙️ Step 2: Sign Up & Add Your Domain

When you sign up, the provider will ask: “What domain do you want to use?” Enter **optionledger.app**. They’ll then ask you to verify ownership of the domain.

### 🔐 Step 3: Verify Domain Ownership (DNS Step)

Log into your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.) and add a **TXT record** provided by your email host.

* **Type:** TXT
* **Name:** @
* **Value:** (Unique string provided by Google/Zoho)

### 📬 Step 4: Set MX Records (The Most Important Part)

MX records tell the internet where your email lives. You’ll replace existing MX records with the ones from your provider. Once set, emails sent to **support@optionledger.app** will route to your provider.

### 👤 Step 5: Create Your Email Address

Inside your provider dashboard, create your users:

* support@optionledger.app
* steve@optionledger.app
* billing@optionledger.app

### 🔁 Optional: Forwarding (The "Cheap" Trick)

If you don’t want to pay, you can use email forwarding from your registrar to send mail to your personal Gmail.

* **⚠️ Downsides:** Harder to send *from* that address and looks less professional as you scale.

### 🧠 Pro Tips for Your App

1. **Use Aliases:** Instead of paying for multiple inboxes, set up `support@` and `billing@` as aliases that all land in your primary seat.
2. **Add SPF + DKIM:** Your provider will give you these TXT/CNAME records. They are essential for preventing your emails from landing in your customers' spam folders.

### 🚀 Recommendation for OptionLedger

Start with **Google Workspace**. Create `support@` and `steve@` (your personal identity). Later, you can integrate these with a helpdesk like Zendesk or Freshdesk.

Perfect — GoDaddy makes this pretty straightforward. I’ll walk you through the exact steps to get **support@optionledger.app** working.

### 🚀 Option A (Recommended): Google Workspace + GoDaddy

**Step 1: Sign up for Google Workspace**

1. Go to Google Workspace and choose a plan (Starter is fine).
2. Enter Business name (**OptionLedger**) and Domain (**optionledger.app**).
3. Create your first user: **steve@optionledger.app** (you can add support@ next).

**Step 2: Verify Domain (GoDaddy)**

Google will give you a **TXT record**. In GoDaddy:

1. Log into GoDaddy → **My Products** → Find **optionledger.app** → Click **DNS**.
2. Under Records, click **Add**.
3. **Type:** TXT | **Name:** @ | **Value:** (paste from Google) | **TTL:** default.
4. Save it, then go back to Google and click **Verify** (can take a few minutes).

**Step 3: Set MX Records (Critical Step)**

Still in GoDaddy DNS:

1. Delete any existing MX records.
2. Click **Add** → choose **MX** and add these one by one (Name = @ for all):
   * **Priority 1:** ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
   * **Priority 5:** ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
   * **Priority 5:** ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
   * **Priority 10:** ALT3.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
   * **Priority 10:** ALT4.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM

**Step 4: Create support@optionledger.app**

In Google Admin Console: Go to **Users** → **Add user** → Name: **Support** | Email: **support@optionledger.app**.

**Step 5: Test It**

Send an email TO **support@optionledger.app** and reply FROM the Gmail interface to ensure it's flowing both ways.

---

### 🧠 Bonus Setup (Highly Recommended)

* **Add SPF (Prevents Spam Flags):** In GoDaddy DNS, add a **TXT record** with **Name:** @ and **Value:** `v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all`.
* **Enable DKIM:** Inside Google Admin (**Apps** → **Gmail** → **Authenticate email**), Google will give you another DNS record to add to GoDaddy. This confirms your identity to other mail servers.

---

### ⚡ Option B (Cheaper): GoDaddy Forwarding Only

If you want a free temporary fix:

1. Go to **GoDaddy** → **Email & Office** → **Email Forwarding**.
2. Create: **support@optionledger.app** → **your Gmail**.

* **⚠️ Note:** You can’t reliably "Send As" this address, and you’ll likely outgrow it quickly as a real app.

### 💡 Final Recommendation

Since you’re building a real product (**OptionLedger**) and already running **Keystone Drone Solutions**, go straight to **Google Workspace**. It provides the professional branding and reliability you'll need as support volume grows.
