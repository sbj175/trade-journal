# OPT-151: P/L Open bar indicator (Tastytrade-style)

## Summary

Added a thin horizontal bar graph to the Open P/L column on the Positions page, inspired by Tastytrade's P/L Open column. The bar visually represents the profit/loss percentage relative to cost basis.

## How It Works

The bar sits at the bottom of the Open P/L cell (4px tall) and has two layers:

1. **Gray track** — a full-width subtle background (`bg-tv-border/40`) that makes the bar visible regardless of P/L direction
2. **Colored fill** — a green or red bar whose width = `abs(openPnL / costBasis) * 100%`, capped at 100%

### Directional behavior

* **Profit (green)**: bar grows from the **left edge** →
* **Loss (red)**: bar grows from the **right edge** ←

This creates an intuitive visual: green fills up from zero, red fills up from the "max loss" side.

## Implementation

Located in `frontend/src/pages/positions/App.vue`, inside the Open P/L column div:

```html
<!-- Open P/L -->
<div class="w-24 text-right text-sm font-medium px-1 py-0.5 relative overflow-hidden"
     style="font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums"
     :class="pnl >= 0 ? 'text-green-300' : 'text-red-300'">
  ${{ formatDollar(pnl) }}
  <!-- P/L bar: gray track -->
  <div class="absolute bottom-0 left-0 right-0 h-[4px] bg-tv-border/40">
    <!-- Colored fill: green grows from left, red grows from right -->
    <div class="absolute top-0 h-full"
         :class="pnl >= 0 ? 'bg-green-500 left-0' : 'bg-red-500 right-0'"
         :style="{ width: Math.min(Math.abs(pnl) / Math.max(Math.abs(costBasis), 1) * 100, 100) + '%' }">
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
```

### Key details

* `relative overflow-hidden` on the outer div contains the absolute-positioned bar
* Gray track uses `bg-tv-border/40` — just visible enough against the dark row background
* Width calculation: `Math.min(abs(openPnL) / max(abs(costBasis), 1) * 100, 100)` — avoids division by zero and caps at 100%
* Tailwind's `left-0` vs `right-0` controls the growth direction
* `h-[4px]` for thickness (adjustable)

## Status

Currently experimental (uncommitted). This issue documents the pattern for future reference.
