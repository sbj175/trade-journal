# OPT-164: Design refresh: bolder, cleaner financial look

Redesign OptionLedger's visual identity with a bolder, clean, professional financial aesthetic.

**Changes:**

* **Color palette**: Deeper backgrounds (#0b0f19 base) with more vivid accent colors (emerald green, vivid red, institutional blue)
* **Typography**: Plus Jakarta Sans (geometric sans) replaces Outfit; IBM Plex Mono (institutional monospace) replaces JetBrains Mono
* **NavBar**: Blue accent line at top, solid blue brand icon with shadow, bolder font weights
* **Component Library**: Updated to reflect new design tokens and font specimens

All pages benefit automatically since Tailwind classes reference `tv-*` design tokens.
