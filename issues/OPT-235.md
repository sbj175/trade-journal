# OPT-235: Pipeline not creating lots for Sell to Open transactions after roll

## Bug

After rolling positions (IBIT calls, MSTR puts) on 2026-03-31, the reprocess pipeline creates 0 lots for the new Sell to Open transactions. Raw transactions are present in the database but the April 10 expiration lots are never created.

## Symptoms

* Positions page shows old expirations (March 31) instead of new (April 10)
* Ledger shows groups as OPEN but with stale expiration dates
* `position_lots` has 0 rows for any `260410` symbols
* Raw transactions for both Buy to Close (old) and Sell to Open (new) are present

## Likely cause

Pipeline order assembly or lot creation may be mishandling same-day close+open pairs from rolls — the Sell to Open may be consumed as a closing action rather than creating a new opening lot.
