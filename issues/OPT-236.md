# OPT-236: Reduce duplicated information between position row and expanded detail panel

## Problem

After adding inline chain P&L sub-rows (OPT-233), the expanded detail panel now duplicates several fields that are already visible on the position row:

* **Cost Basis, Net Liq, Open P&L** — shown in the main row columns AND in the detail panel
* **Net Premium, Chain P&L** — shown in the inline sub-row AND in the detail panel
* **Roll count** — shown in the badge AND in the detail panel

## Proposed approach

Rethink the detail panel

## Closed 2026-05-15

Verified the substantial duplication is resolved in the current
PositionsExpandedPanel.vue:

- **No group-total Cost Basis / Net Liq / Open P&L** in the panel.
  The panel shows per-leg breakdowns instead — useful, not duplicate.
- **No Net Premium or Chain P&L** in the panel (those live in the
  inline sub-row beneath the main row, where they belong).
- One residual "Rolls: N" line near the roll timeline section
  alongside the badge — minor, acceptable as context for the timeline
  below it.

Work happened organically through the design-update refactors
(feature/design-updates-r1 / r2 era). Closing.
